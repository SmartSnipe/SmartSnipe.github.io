About SmartSnipe

At SmartSnipe our top priority is building a product that positively impact hockey players development and help them play to their full potential. There are currently no products on the market that allow a player to practice their shooting that is representitive of real world situations.

SmartSnipe is a smart shooting board that can be attached to any hockey net to immediatly provide a shooter with an experience that simulates real game situations.
