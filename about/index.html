About SmartSnipe

SmartSnipe is a smart shooting board that can be attached to any hockey net to immediatly provide a shooter with an experience that simulates real game situations. The net comes equipped with five slots that open and close to simulate goal scoring opportunities, a radar gun to measure shot speed, and a camera to analyze technique.

Player statistics such as shot speed, reaction time, and accuracy are tracked by the smart shooter tutor and displayed to the user in an iOS application. The user can also configure the training settings for the shooting board using the app.

SmartSnipe comes equipped with a Xbox Kinect. The Kinect will be used for player detection, pose estimation, and stick tracking.
